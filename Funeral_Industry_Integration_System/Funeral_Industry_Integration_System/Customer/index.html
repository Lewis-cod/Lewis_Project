Pre-Planning

Funeral Packages

Funeral pre-planning involves arranging funeral details in advance. It includes selecting burial or cremation options, choosing a funeral service type, and outlining personal preferences. Pre-planning eases the emotional and financial burden on loved ones. It ensures wishes are respected and provides peace of mind.

Buddhist

Funeral Packages

A Buddhist funeral emphasizes peace and the cycle of rebirth. It often includes chanting, prayers, and meditation to guide the deceased's spirit. Offerings like flowers, incense, and food are made to honor the deceased. Monks may lead ceremonies and deliver teachings on impermanence. Simplicity and respect are central to the rituals.

Taoist

Funeral Packages

A Taoist funeral reflects harmony with nature and the universe. Rituals involve chanting, music, and offerings to guide the soul to the afterlife. Priests perform ceremonies to ward off evil spirits and ensure peace for the deceased. Paper money, goods, and symbolic items are burned for use in the spirit world. Family and community participation are key to honoring the deceased.

Christian

Funeral Packages

A Christian funeral focuses on faith, hope, and eternal life. It often includes prayers, hymns, and scripture readings. The service reflects the deceased's life and celebrates their journey to be with God. Burial or cremation follows, with a graveside service or committal. Comfort, remembrance, and faith in resurrection are central themes.
